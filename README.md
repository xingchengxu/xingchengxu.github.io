My name is Xingcheng Xu, and I am a research scientist at [Shanghai AI Laboratory](https://www.shlab.org.cn/). Previously, I worked as a data scientist at Ant Group and Alibaba Group. I earned my Ph.D. from the School of Mathematical Sciences at Peking University, where I was also a joint Ph.D. student at the Mathematical Institute, University of Oxford.

My research interests are focused on the intersection of probability theory, artificial intelligence and their applications. I am dedicated to exploring this interdisciplinary space and uncovering novel insights and practical applications.
